About Tejas Manufacture

Established in 2022, Tejas Manufacture is a fast-growing manufacturer of advanced industrial equipment based in Baraut, Uttar Pradesh. We are proud to deliver robust, reliable, and efficient engineering solutions that support industries in air pollution control, material handling, and process flow management.

With a commitment to quality, customization, and customer satisfaction, Tejas Manufacture has emerged as a trusted partner to businesses across India. Our focus is on manufacturing cutting-edge dust collectors, elevators, conveyors, and other heavy-duty industrial machinery.

Whether you're in cement, chemical, pharmaceuticals, food processing, or metal fabrication, we have the equipment to meet your unique operational needs.
